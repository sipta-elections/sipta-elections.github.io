Welcome to the website for the SIPTA Elections 2021!


You can find the SIPTA by-laws, according to which these elections are organised, here.


There are 5 offices (President, Secretary, Treasurer, Executive editor, and At-large member):

For the office of President, there is 1 candidate, of which 1 must be elected: Jasper De Bock.

For the office of Secretary, there is 1 candidate, of which 1 must be elected: Enrique Miranda.

For the office of Treasurer, there is 1 candidate, of which 1 must be elected: Arianna Casanova.

For the office of Executive editor, there are 3 candidates, of which 1 must be elected: Alexander Erreygers, Cassio De Campos and Erik Quaeghebeur.

For At-large members, there are 10 candidates, of which 5 must be elected: Alexander Erreygers, Cassio de Campos, Enrique Miranda, Erik Quaeghebeur, Hector Diego Estrada Lugo, Henna Bains, Jasper de Bock, Nawapon Nakharutai, Ryan Martin and Ullrika Sahlin.


The voting period is from 10 May 2021 till 10 June 2021.


In case of any questions, please contact here.
