Welcome to the website for the SIPTA Elections 2019!


You can find the SIPTA by-laws, according to which these elections are organised, here.


There are 5 offices (President, Secretary, Treasurer, Executive editor, and At-large member):

For the office of President, there is 1 candidate, of which 1 must be elected: Jasper De Bock.

For the office of Secretary, there is 1 candidate, of which 1 must be elected: Matthias Troffaes.

For the office of Treasurer, there is 1 candidate, of which 1 must be elected: Alessandro Antonucci.

For the office of Executive editor, there are 2 candidates, of which 1 must be elected: Alexander Erreygers and Ignacio Montes.

For At-large members, there are 9 candidates, of which 5 must be elected: Jasper de Bock, Cassio de Campos, Gert de Cooman, Sébastien Destercke, Alexander Erreygers, Enrique Miranda, Ignacio Montes, Ullrika Sahlin and Georg Schollmeyer.


The voting period is from 10 May 2019 till 10 June 2019.


In case of any questions, please contact here.
